Crew Fired

A weapon that is Crew Fired benefits from a 360-degree Arc of Fire and does not need to declare a facing when purchased.

Gas Grenades

If this weapon scores one or more un-cancelled hits on the target, instead of causing damage, reduce the target's Crew Value by 1 for each un-cancelled hit, to a minimum of 0, until the end of the Gear Phase.

Steel Nets

This weapon's hits do not cause damage. Hits will add Hazard Tokens as a result of the Blast special rule as normal.

Shotgun

When attacking with this weapon, roll 3D6 attack dice if the target is within Short Range, 2D6 attack dice if the target is within Medium Range, and 1D6 attack dice if the target is within Long Range.
